About Enroute Store

Delivering Premium Digital Products for the BUSSID Community

Our Story

Enroute Store was born from a deep passion for the Bus Simulator Indonesia (BUSSID) community. What started as a small project by dedicated gaming enthusiasts has grown into a trusted destination for premium digital mods — serving thousands of players across India and beyond.

We noticed a gap in the market: players wanted high-quality, realistic mods but struggled to find reliable sources. That's why we built Enroute Store — a platform that brings together the finest crafted vehicles, maps, and game modifications under one roof, with a seamless and secure purchasing experience.

Our Mission

At Enroute Store, our mission is simple: to make premium gaming experiences accessible to everyone. We are committed to offering the highest quality digital products, ensuring instant delivery, and providing a secure, hassle-free shopping experience for every customer.

We specialize in curating and delivering premium BUSSID mods, including:
