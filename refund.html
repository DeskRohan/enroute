Return & Refund Policy

Understanding our policy for digital product purchases

Last updated: June 13, 2026

Thank you for shopping at EnrouteIn. We appreciate your business and want to ensure you have a clear understanding of our return and refund policy for all digital products purchased through our platform.

1. Digital Products — No Returns

All products sold on EnrouteIn are digital goods (BUSSID mods, vehicle models, maps, skins, etc.). Due to the nature of digital products:

  • Digital products cannot be "returned" once the download link has been provided or the file has been accessed.
  • All sales are considered final upon successful payment and delivery of the download link.
  • We encourage you to carefully review the product description, images, and specifications before making a purchase.

2. Eligible Refund Scenarios

While all sales are generally final, we understand that certain situations may warrant a refund. We will consider refund requests in the following cases:

✅ Eligible for Refund

  • Wrong Product Delivered: You received a different product than what you ordered.
  • Non-Delivery: Payment was successfully processed, but the download link was never made available on your dashboard.
  • Duplicate Charge: You were charged multiple times for the same product.
  • Corrupted File: The downloaded file is corrupted and non-functional, and we are unable to provide a working replacement.
  • Product Significantly Different: The product is substantially different from what was described on the product page.

❌ Not Eligible for Refund

  • You changed your mind after purchase.
  • You purchased the wrong product by mistake (please contact us — we may be able to exchange it).
  • The product does not work due to an incompatible device or game version.
  • You claim the product was not as expected without specific evidence.
  • The download link has already been accessed or the file has been downloaded.

3. Refund Request Process

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact Us: Send an email to enroutestorein@gmail.com with the subject line "Refund Request — [Your Order ID]".
  2. Provide Details: Include the following information:
    • Your registered email address
    • Order ID / Transaction ID
    • Product name
    • Reason for the refund request
    • Any supporting screenshots or evidence
  3. Review: Our team will review your request within 3–5 business days and respond with the outcome.
  4. Refund Processing: If approved, the refund will be processed back to your original payment method within 7–10 business days.

4. Refund Amount

  • Approved refunds will be for the full purchase amount of the product.
  • Payment gateway charges or processing fees (if any) may be deducted from the refund amount.
  • Refunds will be credited to the same payment method used for the original purchase.

5. Cancellations

Since our products are digital and delivery is instant, cancellations are not possible once the payment has been processed and the download link has been generated. If you experience issues during payment, please contact us immediately.

6. Exchanges

We do not offer direct exchanges. However, if you purchased the wrong product by mistake, please contact our support team within 24 hours of purchase. We will do our best to assist you on a case-by-case basis.

7. Disputes

If you are not satisfied with the outcome of your refund request, you may escalate the matter by contacting us directly. We are committed to resolving all disputes amicably and fairly.
